## Anti-Patterns Verdict

One absolute-ban tell is present and pervasive: the 3px `border-left` colored
side-stripe via `@mixin accent-border` (`main.scss:99-101`), applied to
`.card` (:279), `code`/`pre` via `%code-base` (:214), `blockquote` (:193),
and `.side-content` at <=768px (:294). Confirmed in compiled output by the
FJ#1 deterministic detector (`styles.css:18`). No gradient text, no
glassmorphism, no hero-metric, no identical-card-grid. The `hover-shadow`
mixin (`:95-97`) puts a `0 3px 8px` drop shadow on chip hover (`:329,344,514`)
where DESIGN.md's elevation model specifies a contact *glow* — an
Emanation-Over-Elevation violation, not an AI tell but a spec contradiction.
Apart from the side-stripe the system is distinctive and intentional; this is
not a generic AI aesthetic, it is one banned pattern applied widely.

## Executive Summary

- **Audit Health Score: 12/20** (Acceptable; accessibility is the weak axis at 2/4)
- **Issues by severity:** P0 ×1 (cross-referenced, owned by FJ#3) · P1 ×5 · P2 ×4 · P3 ×3
- **Top findings:**
  1. **Contrast resolved + one new failure.** Every body, heading, and inline
     accent passes AA on both the Nightshade ground and the card surface.
     The critique's open worry (magenta `strong` / gold `em` on deep purple)
     **passes**: magenta on ground 4.77:1, gold 7.25:1, teal 6.27:1, Ghost
     body 17.84:1. But the magenta chip label on its own `rgba(accent,0.15)`
     tint computes to **4.41:1, failing WCAG 1.4.3 AA** for the 0.8rem chip
     text. The critique could not catch this; the audit's math did.
  2. **No `prefers-reduced-motion` anywhere** (`main.scss`, zero occurrences).
     The single `transition: all 0.2s ease` (`:107-109`, applied to every `a`
     and `%tag-base`) and the `glow()` text-shadows on `h1` (`:159`) and link
     hover (`:187`) are ungated. DESIGN.md marks this required, not optional.
  3. **No focus-visible styling at all.** No `:focus`, `:focus-visible`, or
     `outline` rule in the entire stylesheet. `a` sets only `:hover` and
     carries `text-decoration:none` with a custom border, so keyboard focus
     falls back to the UA default outline alone on a dark ground. WCAG 2.4.7
     (AA).
  4. **No skip-link.** No bypass mechanism into `<main>` (`baseof.html:18`).
     WCAG 2.4.1 Bypass Blocks (A).
  5. **No body measure cap + sub-44px touch targets.** Reading line length is
     unbounded on wide screens; chips/pagination links are ~25px tall.

## Detailed Findings by Severity

### [P0] 3px accent-border side-stripe (hard ban) — cross-referenced
- **Location:** `main.scss:99-101` (mixin); applied `:193, :214, :279, :294`
- **Category:** Anti-Pattern
- **Impact:** Pervasive banned pattern on every card, code block, blockquote;
  amplified on mobile (`.side-content` gains it only at <=768px) where most
  of the audience is.
- **Standard:** impeccable absolute ban (side-stripe borders)
- **Recommendation:** Owned by FJ#3. Delete the mixin; replace with full 1px
  hairlines or background-tint shifts; code/pre on the Glyph Well wash alone.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P1] Magenta chip label fails WCAG AA contrast
- **Location:** `.response-tag` (`main.scss:337-340`), `.status-link`
  (`:500-504`); label `color(accent)` on `rgba(accent,0.15)` over card
- **Category:** Accessibility
- **Impact:** "View all scrolls" / reply-status chips: 0.8rem label at
  **4.41:1**, below the 4.5:1 normal-text minimum. The gold `.tag`
  (`rgba(secondary,0.2)`) is fine at 5.80:1; only the magenta chips fail.
- **Standard:** WCAG 2.1 SC 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum), AA
- **Recommendation:** Raise the magenta label toward white, lighten the chip
  background, or increase label size past the large-text boundary. A design
  decision for FJ#3, not a mechanical bump.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable colorize` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P1] No prefers-reduced-motion gating
- **Location:** `main.scss` global; `transition()` `:107-109`, `glow()`
  `:87-89`, applied `:159, :183, :187, :318`
- **Category:** Accessibility / Anti-Pattern
- **Impact:** Glow and all transitions fire unconditionally for users who
  request reduced motion. DESIGN.md / PRODUCT.md call this non-negotiable.
- **Standard:** WCAG 2.3.3 (AAA) + project hard requirement
- **Recommendation:** Wrap the page in a `@media (prefers-reduced-motion:
  reduce)` block that neutralizes `transition` and `text-shadow` on the
  affected selectors. Gate at the mixin call sites.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P1] No visible focus indicator
- **Location:** `main.scss` global (no `:focus`/`:focus-visible`/`outline`)
- **Category:** Accessibility
- **Impact:** Keyboard users get only the unstyled UA outline on a deep-purple
  ground; links have `text-decoration:none` so focus is easily lost.
- **Standard:** WCAG 2.1 SC 2.4.7 Focus Visible, AA
- **Recommendation:** Add a `:focus-visible` treatment consistent with the
  link-glow language (solid teal border + teal glow), distinct from `:hover`.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P1] No skip-link / bypass block
- **Location:** `layouts/_default/baseof.html` (no skip anchor before `:18`)
- **Category:** Accessibility
- **Impact:** Keyboard/SR users cannot bypass the header and side scroll to
  reach the article.
- **Standard:** WCAG 2.1 SC 2.4.1 Bypass Blocks, A
- **Recommendation:** Add a visually-hidden-until-focused skip link targeting
  `<main>`.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P1] No body measure cap — cross-referenced (FJ#1 typeset)
- **Location:** no ch/rem `max-width` on any prose container
- **Category:** Responsive / Readability
- **Impact:** ~1100px line length on a wide display vs the 65-75ch spec, on
  the site's primary surface. Already FJ#1 P1; re-confirmed at code level.
- **Standard:** DESIGN.md / PRODUCT.md typography
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable typeset` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P2] Interactive targets below 44px
- **Location:** `%tag-base` `:311-320` (`padding: 3px 10px`, 0.8rem),
  `.pagination .page` `:400-403`
- **Category:** Responsive / Accessibility
- **Impact:** Tags, response/status chips, and pagination numbers compute to
  ~25px tall; short labels can fall under the 24px AA minimum. The audience
  is mobile-heavy.
- **Standard:** WCAG 2.5.8 Target Size (Minimum) AA (24px); 44px guideline
- **Recommendation:** Increase chip vertical padding / min-height; enlarge
  pagination hit area.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable adapt` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P2] Emanation-Over-Elevation violation on chip hover
- **Location:** `hover-shadow` `:95-97`, applied `:329, :344, :514`
- **Category:** Anti-Pattern (spec contradiction)
- **Impact:** Drop shadow where DESIGN.md specifies an accent-tinted contact
  glow; reads as "lifted" not "emanating."
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P2] Semantic structure: nav not a landmark, dual h1
- **Location:** `baseof.html` (Teleportation Circle is `.side-content`, not
  `<nav>`), `header.html:2` h1 + post-single h1 = two h1 per article page
- **Category:** Accessibility
- **Impact:** Screen-reader landmark navigation is weaker than it could be;
  flat heading hierarchy (also FJ#1 critique).
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P2] Token-layer misuse: spacing token as font-size
- **Location:** `main.scss:402` `.page { font-size: space(xl) }` (= 20px)
- **Category:** Theming
- **Impact:** Type sizing bypasses `$font-sizes`; brittle, off the type ramp.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable typeset` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P3] `transition: all` is imprecise
- **Location:** `main.scss:107-109` default arg
- **Category:** Performance
- **Impact:** Animated props here are paint-only so no thrash today, but
  `all` will animate any future property including layout ones. Scope it to
  `border-color, box-shadow, text-shadow`.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable optimize` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P3] Hard-coded color outside the token system
- **Location:** `main.scss:121` inline SVG data-URI fill `%23B5A693` @ 0.06
- **Category:** Theming
- **Impact:** The only color not flowing through `$colors`; ash-glyph hue is
  invisible to any future palette change.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden` (within FJ#3 craft)

### [P3] Unverified asset weight
- **Location:** webpack bundle (Phosphor Icons, PureCSS, Atkinson families)
- **Category:** Performance
- **Impact:** Phosphor full set + multiple font families can be heavy on the
  mobile-heavy, possibly-slow-connection audience. Not measurable from source
  alone; verify the icon subset and bundle size during FJ#3.
- **Suggested command:** `/impeccable optimize` (within FJ#3 craft)

## Patterns & Systemic Issues

- **Motion and focus accessibility were never wired.** No reduced-motion, no
  focus-visible, no skip-link: a single systemic gap (interaction-state
  accessibility) rather than three isolated misses. One coherent workstream.
- **Accent-on-accent contrast is the blind spot.** Accents on the ground were
  clearly designed for; accents on their own tinted chips were not checked.
  Only the magenta 0.15 tint crosses the line, but the pattern (label color
  == accent, bg == same accent at low alpha) is the systemic shape to fix.
- **Token system is strong but leaks at two points** (spacing-as-font-size,
  hard-coded SVG hue). Minor, but they are exactly the seams the keystone
  forbids.

## Positive Findings

- Body text contrast is exceptional (17-19:1); the legibility backbone is real.
- Genuine token architecture (`$colors/$fonts/$spacing/$font-sizes` + accessor
  functions), used symbolically throughout. Maintainable and theme-coherent.
- Effects are paint-only (text-shadow/box-shadow), tiny tiled inline-SVG
  ground, no blur/filter, no layout animation. Performance posture is sound.
- Correct `lang`, `charset`, zoomable `viewport`, `<main>` landmark,
  `aria-hidden` on decorative spacer columns. The semantic baseline exists.
- Responsive scaffolding (PureCSS grid, 768px breakpoint, fluid images,
  mobile reflow) is coherent.
